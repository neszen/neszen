# Neszen

**Backend Stability & Scale Readiness Consulting**

We help growing product companies identify hidden backend risks before they become outages, rewrites, or revenue loss.

---

### What We Do

We run structured **Backend Stability & Scale Readiness Reviews** — focused diagnostics that tell founders exactly where their systems will break under growth, why, and what to fix first.

This is not implementation work. This is independent system diagnosis.

**We evaluate:**

- Architecture fragility & failure modes
- Database bottlenecks & scaling constraints
- Async processing risks & race conditions
- Cloud cost explosion triggers
- Observability & operational readiness gaps
- Technical debt reaching critical mass

Every review ends with a clear verdict: **Stable, Risky, or Unstable** — tied to business impact, not just technical opinion.

---

### Who This Is For

- Post-MVP founders preparing to scale
- SaaS companies with growing backend complexity
- Technical leaders who need an independent second opinion
- Teams where every release feels risky and hotfixes are routine

---

### Tech Context

Python-first backend systems — Django, FastAPI, Flask · PostgreSQL, Redis · Celery · JWT & secure auth · AWS, Docker, CI/CD · Event-driven & async architectures · LLM infrastructure

---

### How We Work

1. **Context capture** — Understand business reality, growth trajectory, and risk profile
2. **Deep review** — Architecture, stability, scalability, cost, and operations analysis
3. **Findings report** — Written risk map with prioritized recommendations
4. **Clear verdict** — Actionable decision on what to fix now, what can wait, and what to leave alone
