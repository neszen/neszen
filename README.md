# Neszen

**Production Python Backends · Secure APIs · Applied AI**

We design, build, and operate production Python backends for growing product companies — and integrate AI into them with the same discipline.

---

### What We Do

We build backend systems that stay reliable as they grow — production-grade Python, secure APIs, and applied AI integrated into real business workflows, not demos.

Most engagements start with a **Backend Stability & Scale Readiness Review**: a focused diagnostic that tells founders exactly where their systems will break under growth, why, and what to fix first. From there, when you want it done, we're the team that builds and operates the fix — not just the report.

**We evaluate and harden:**
- Architecture fragility & failure modes
- Database bottlenecks & scaling constraints
- Async processing risks & race conditions
- Cloud cost explosion triggers
- Observability & operational readiness gaps
- Technical debt reaching critical mass
- AI integration points — where LLM features touch production systems

Every review ends with a clear verdict: **Stable, Risky, or Unstable** — tied to business impact, not just technical opinion.
